Project Proposal

Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to have a site where customers can learn more about the “Save” company and what they do. This company sells custom t-shirts and donates money to the needy through their merchandise. Through this website, customers will be able to buy shirts and other merchandise in order to give money to a good cause. Customers will also be able to get in contact with the Save team for any business inquiries. This will be an ending point for the company as most of the advertising will be done through social media. Customers will come to the site to learn more and to buy merchandise.

The Audience

The audience for the save company is young adults ages 17-26. This audience wants simple, quick information given. They want to see products and prices fast and they want to know what their money is used for as well. They want to be able to relate to the personalities of the save company owners, so gaining customer trust will be a priority. Trust will be gained by the customers by using pictures of the staff throughout the website, doing good. This audience is most likely in high school or college and they have a lot of connection to social media. This website will be the endpoint for customers that were advertised to through social media to buy the product

Web Pages

  1. Home: The homepage will be simple and updated often, it will have the company’s latest events, clothes as well as general information about the company. There will also be links to the company’s social media sites on the main page and pictures of the staff.
  2. Our story: this page will explain how the company got started and what they are all about. It will explain what they do and why they started to do it. It will also inspire readers to buy product from the company
  3. Shop: This page will have pictures and prices of the merchandise for the store. Customers will be able to pick the product that they want and find out how much it is for them to ship the product as well. There will be information on how to requiest an email invoice, so they can purchase the products
  4. Gallery: This page will be full of images of the staff and the service that they provide through the save company. It will be a place to display the good that the company is contributing to the world
  5. Contact: this page will have contact information such as email and additional social media information. There will be a place that the customer can contact then directly through the website

Usability Concepts

This website will be simple and clean. Most of it will have a white background and sans serif black titles that are easily read. Margins will be kept big so that the site appears thin on a browser. There will be contrasting colors including blue and red. All the images will be stored in the repository for fast loading.

The company logo and slogan will be placed at the top and throughout the website. The business identity will relate to customers since it is a company created by college students, aiming towards college students

Jquery modules will be included in the site for consistent navigation bar and footer. There will be few dropdown menus so that it is easy to navigate. The navigation titles will be one word each so that it is easy for the customer to know what each page does. There will also be a hover effect and change of color when consumers are on certain pages so that they know which page they are currently on.

The main headings will be with sans serif, thin lettering and short. The body copy will consist of short paragraphs, when needed. For the most part, a lot of the information will be shown through pictures so that the information doesn’t look overwhelming to read and the customers will be able to get the information fast.
